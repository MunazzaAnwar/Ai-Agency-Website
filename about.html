our story

Engineers and designers who'd rather build than talk about building.

Pulse Labs started in 2020 as a two-person freelance team solving backend problems for fintech startups. Today we're a 48-person studio shipping AI products and platforms for companies across 14 countries.

who we are

Senior by default, not by exception.

Every Pulse Labs engagement is staffed with people who've shipped production software before — not a bench of juniors learning on your budget. We keep teams small, decisions fast, and scope honest.

We work as an extension of your team, not a vendor at arm's length. That means shared Slack channels, shared standups, and shared accountability for the outcome — not just the output.

what drives us

Four things we won't compromise on

◆

Craft over speed

We move fast, but never by cutting corners on architecture, accessibility, or code quality.

▣

Radical transparency

You see the backlog, the burn rate, and the blockers — every sprint, no filtered status reports.

☁

Outcomes, not hours

We measure ourselves by what shipped and what it moved, not by timesheets.

✦

Design is engineering

Designers and engineers sit in the same sprint, not in a handoff queue six weeks apart.
